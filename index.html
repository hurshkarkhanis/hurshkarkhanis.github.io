About

Hey!

I'm Hursh, a recent graduate from the University of San Francisco, where I majored in finance and minored in computer science. There are two reasons I started my website:

First off, I felt it would be a great place for my programming portfolio, which list some of the data related projects I have built both in school as well as in my free time.

Secondly, I love writing. I've written extensively for my college newspaper and I have always found that it has come natural to me. As I have gotten older and taken charge of my personal finances, I figured starting to write about them would be both a fun and informative project to pursue.

Enjoy!
